InfiSpark Innovation Academy

InfiSpark Innovation Academy provides a personalized learning environment for the student of K12. Utilizing digital learning tools and relevant project-based instruction approaches, the academy personalizes education for students by addressing distinct learning needs, interests, and aspirations of individual students. In this personalized learning environment, the students move from a “one-style-fits-all” approach to student-centred learning.

What do we offer?

  • Build your own ROBOTS! Learn the fundamentals of Robotics using famous LEGO kit for Robotics.

  • Discover the power of RASPBERRY PI. Learn to program with Scratch and acquire knowledge about Raspberry Pi & single board computers.

  • Learn to build your own MOBILE APPLICATION. Build your first application on windows platform and create its user interface the easy way.

  • Learn AR & VR by experiencing! Get familiar with Augmented, Virtual & Mixed Reality. Build your own application in Unity 3D and deploy it on your own favourite device.

The Innovation Academy is founded in the strong foundation of core education instruction through the use of new century skills and innovative learning strategies. Competency measures demonstrate student success as they master skills. In creating a competency-based environment, students will apply lessons and skills by engaging in real-world, authentic problems and learning strategies to become independent and innovative thinkers. These strategies are the keys an ever-changing world focused on student knowledge and the ability to work creatively to find solutions in society and in the workplace.

Why choose InfiSpark Innovation Academy?

  • Technology to transform the learning environment beyond the walls of a typical classroom and a traditionally defined school day schedule.

  • A blended learning approach to complete skills through the mentorship of teachers.

  • Competency-based curriculum to master competencies to move on to the next lesson.

  • Project based skills application.

InfiSpark StartUp Engagement Program

InfiSpark Startup Engagement Program connects you with the best global startups specific to your needs or challenge.Unlike other programs, InfiSpark StartUp Engagement Program goes beyond just connecting startups to a fully tailored, one-to-one engagement model. InfiSpark works with you to identify opportunities with startups and guides you through the engagement process to facilitate the successful and measurable outcome.

What does the program look like?

  • StartUp Discovery.Finding the potential startups that can add value to the campus.

  • Connecting With Campuses.Allowing campuses to connect with StartUp Founders and encouraging interactions between students and startup founders and strengthening the relationship with startup ecosystem.

  • Project AllocationOpportunities for students help in the development of products as well as the marketing and business development strategies.

  • Internships/Job Opportunities Opportunities for students to work as an intern in startups and even get hired.

Why chose InfiSpark StartUp Engagement Program?

  • We have an unparalleled track record with top technology startups

  • We are connected to startup ecosystems around the world, not just India.

  • Our proprietary pilot design and framework result in more effective partnerships.
