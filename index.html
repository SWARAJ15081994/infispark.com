InfiSpark is a venture focused on emerging technologies like machine learning, robotics, augmented reality and virtual reality, IoT etc., engaging with startups, developers and student ecosystem. We engage with communities in building strategic engagements which includes training, consulting and project development during various events like hackathons, coding fests, workshops etc.

InfiSpark provides a holistic range of Technology Learning Solutions which makes acquisition of skills easier. We offer an amalgamation of technology and education through nonconventional ways of learning.

At InfiSpark we believe in transforming regular course content and relating it to real-life examples enabling students to learn from their experiences.
